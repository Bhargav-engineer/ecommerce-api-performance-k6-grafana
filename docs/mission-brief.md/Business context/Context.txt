What is this system?
A cars buying guide site which contains car booking, car searching, buget, reviews, customer experiences

Who are the users?
End users on web/mobile clients searching and decide to buy cars.

What matters for them?
They care that search/browse feel fast and booking does not fail or hang.

What is the basic traffic expectation?
We assume a peak of maybe 50–100 requests per second across search+book in the first version; we’ll tune this later as we model.

Why are we doing performance work?
Goal is to keep checkout latency and error rate within agreed SLOs so we don’t lose sales when traffic spikes.
